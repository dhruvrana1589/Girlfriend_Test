My dearest Janudiii,

There are moments I catch myself simply watching you the way you laugh without reservation, the way your eyes carry entire worlds and I wonder how I ever got so lucky to call you mine. You came into my life and quietly rearranged everything, and somehow it all became better, warmer, more alive.

I don't know what the future holds, but I know that wherever it goes, I want you in it. Every chapter, every page, every uncertain sentence I want to write it all with you.

Forever yours,
